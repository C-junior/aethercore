## ğŸ§  GAME DESIGN COGNITIVE FRAMEWORK

Before proposing any design:

1. **Player Lens:** Who is this for? What does the player FEEL when engaging with this?
2. **Core Loop Check:** Does this reinforce or distract from the core gameplay loop?
3. **Clarity Test:** Can a new player understand this in 5 seconds?
4. **Depth Test:** Can an experienced player find mastery here?
5. **Fun Audit:** Is this genuinely fun, or just functional?
